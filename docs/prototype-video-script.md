# Prototype Video Script

Use this as the final recording guide for Loom, YouTube unlisted, or Vimeo.

## Target Length

2 to 4 minutes

## Script

### 1. Opening

`Hi, we are Paystash Labs, and this is Oneto, an offline-capable payment product designed to make transactions work even when there is no immediate internet connection at the point of payment.`

### 2. Problem

`In many payment scenarios, a transaction fails simply because one or both users do not have reliable connectivity at that exact moment. That creates friction, failed checkouts, and poor payment reliability.`

### 3. Solution

`Oneto solves this by allowing a student to generate a signed offline payment intent on-device, share it through a QR code, and let the merchant reconcile the payment later when internet becomes available again.`

### 4. Demo Flow

`In this demo, the student account is running on a physical Android device using the production APK, while the merchant side is shown on an iOS device using Expo Go.`

`First, we show student onboarding. Then we show merchant onboarding. After that, the student selects a merchant, enters an amount, confirms the payment, and the app generates a signed QR code.`

`The merchant scans the QR code and stores the payment for later reconciliation. Once the merchant is back online, the payment can be submitted to the backend for verification and reconciliation.`

### 5. Korapay Integration

`For online money movement, Oneto integrates Korapay for top-ups and merchant cashout-related flows, giving the system a practical bridge between offline payment intent and online settlement operations.`

### 6. Why It Matters

`This supports simplified payments and financial inclusion by reducing dependence on immediate connectivity and making digital payments more usable in low-connectivity environments.`

### 7. Close

`This is Oneto by Paystash Labs. Thank you.`

## Recording Tips

- Show the mobile app first, not the admin panel
- Keep the cursor and taps deliberate and slow
- Avoid long setup steps in the recording
- If a live flow is unstable, narrate a prepared happy path instead of forcing a broken demo
